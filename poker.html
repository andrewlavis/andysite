So, you think you might want to play poker. If you can avoid it, I definitely would as the game is guaranteed to shave about 5 years off your life. On this page I'll include some resources and tips from one of the best poker players in my house.

Top 5 Poker Tips

  1. Value bet your strong hands
  2. Be consistent with the story you are trying to tell when making a raise
  3. Read your cards carefully
  4. Expect to get coolered on the river by someone who has no idea how to play
  5. Don't bother playing poker, you can avoid a lot of frustration this way
